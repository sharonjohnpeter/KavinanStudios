Our Process

A clear, transparent four-stage journey from first conversation to final handover.

01

Discovery

Understanding your vision, requirements, lifestyle, and budget. We begin with an in-depth consultation to learn about your needs, preferences, and aspirations for the space.

  • Initial consultation & site visit
  • Requirement analysis & budget planning
  • Feasibility study & preliminary assessment
02

Design Development

Concept creation, planning, visualizations, and material selection. We transform your ideas into visual concepts with detailed drawings and 3D renderings.

  • Mood boards & concept development
  • 3D visualizations & walkthroughs
  • Material selection & color palette
03

Execution Planning

Detailed coordination, timelines, costing, and project scheduling. We create a comprehensive roadmap for seamless project execution.

  • Detailed BOQ & cost estimation
  • Project timeline & milestone planning
  • Vendor & contractor coordination
04

Build & Delivery

Execution, supervision, quality checks, and final handover. We bring the design to life with meticulous attention to detail and quality control.

  • On-site supervision & quality checks
  • Regular progress updates & reviews
  • Final inspection & project handover

Every Stage Explained

01

Discovery Phase

We begin by understanding your vision, lifestyle needs, functional requirements, and budget constraints. This includes site visits, measurements, and detailed discussions about your expectations.

Deliverables: Project brief, initial sketches, budget estimate
02

Design Development

Our creative team develops concept designs, space plans, and 3D visualizations. We present multiple design options and refine based on your feedback until perfection.

Deliverables: 3D renderings, material boards, design drawings
03

Execution Planning

Detailed project planning including Bill of Quantities (BOQ), contractor selection, timeline creation, and procurement scheduling for materials and fixtures.

Deliverables: Detailed BOQ, project schedule, vendor list

On-site execution with regular supervision, quality checks, and progress updates. We ensure adherence to design specifications and timelines until final handover.

Deliverables: Completed project, warranty documents, maintenance guide
common questions

Process FAQs

How long does the entire process take?

Project duration varies based on scope and complexity. A residential interior typically takes 2-3 months, while a full architectural project may take 6-12 months. We provide a detailed timeline during the discovery phase.

Can I make changes during the process?

Absolutely! We encourage feedback and refinements during the design phase. Changes during execution may impact timeline and cost, which we communicate transparently.

How do you ensure quality control?

We have multi-level quality checks at every stage - material inspections, workmanship reviews, and final walkthroughs with our quality assurance team.

What is included in your turnkey solution?

Our turnkey solution covers everything from design to execution - architectural work, interiors, furniture, fixtures, electrical, plumbing, and final styling. You just move in!
