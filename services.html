common questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide services outside Kumbakonam?

Yes, we serve clients across Tamil Nadu including Thanjavur, Trichy, Chennai, and other major cities. We also take up select projects outside Tamil Nadu.

What is the typical project timeline?

Timeline varies based on project scope. A residential interior typically takes 2-3 months, while a full architectural project may take 6-12 months.

Do you offer only design or full execution?

Both! We offer design-only consultations as well as complete turnkey solutions from concept to handover. Choose what works best for you.

How do I get a quote for my project?

Simply fill out our contact form or call us. We'll schedule a site visit and provide a detailed quotation within 5-7 business days.
